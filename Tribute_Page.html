Sappho

Poet, icon, lover

Things you may not know about her:

She was a pretty interesting charachter. Many know her as an early Greek poet, but there is much else to her:
    She was called "The tenth muse" and "The poetess" in ancient times. Most of her poetry did not actually survive to our times. Not much is known abouther life - but we do know she was exiled to Sicily. She is the origin of the words "sapphic" and "lesbian".
