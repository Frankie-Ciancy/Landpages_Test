Sappho

Poet, icon, lover

Things you may not know about her:

She was a pretty interesting character. Many know her as an early Greek poet, but there is much else to her:

  • She was called "The tenth muse" and "The poetess" in ancient times.
  • Most of her poetry did not actually survive to our times.
  • Not much is known about her life - but we do know she was exiled to Sicily.
  • She is the origin of the words "sapphic" and "lesbian".

To know more about her, feel free to check out Sappho's Wikipedia page.
